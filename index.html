welcome to
an exhibition on decoding how qr codes work and interact with us
what decode is about
QR codes are everywhere — on walls, screens, receipts, and menus — yet we rarely pause to think about how they work. This exhibition invites you to slow down and explore the hidden design behind these everyday squares.
what you can expect
Through a series of 7 interactive stations, you’ll learn how QR codes see, correct, adapt, and communicate. Each task challenges you to make a code scannable, revealing something new about how technology reads the world, and how we, in turn, read technology.
