what you just experienced
Every QR code has three position markers that help scanners detect the code instantly — no matter its size, rotation, or angle.

The 3 large squares (finder patterns) tell the algorithm where the corners of the code are, while the smaller alignment and timing patterns fine-tune distortion. This universal geometry makes QR codes readable anywhere in the world.
